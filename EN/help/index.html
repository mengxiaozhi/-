How does it work?

With the rise of the Covid-19 epidemic, Miaoli National Ministry of Health, Labour and Welfare launched this system to ensure the safety of the people.

This system will be located according to the data exchange of Bluetooth and Wi-Fi hot spots in Miaoli. After the relevant data is imported into the Ministry of Health, Labour and Welfare, the National Health Insurance will locate individuals. If the data is not abnormal, a health certificate can be obtained on this platform.

The data of this system is supervised by the Network Security Committee of the public welfare consortium legal person. It is protected by the General Data Protection Law of Miaoli State. Except for the Ministry of Health, Labour and Welfare, there is no access permission. Please rest assured to use it.
